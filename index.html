Short Courses

Investment Advisors and Stockbrokers (IASBC)

This premier professional competence course is offered in collaboration with the Securities Exchange Commission (SEC) & Lusaka Securities Exchange (LuSE) as a prerequisite for licensing by the SEC .

APPLY NOW

Pensions Administration and Management (CCPAM)

This competence course is offered in collaboration with the Zambia Associatiion of Pension Funds (ZAPF) for professionals in the pensions sector. It carries substantive CPD hours for participants and members of the Association.

APPLY NOW

Logistics & Transport Competence Course (CILT)

This premier professional competence course is offered in collaboration with the Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transport (CILT) and is designed as a prerequisite for certification and membership with CILT, empowering professionals in the logistics and transport sector with globally recognized standards.

APPLY NOW

Motor Assessors Competence Course (MA)

This essential professional course is designed to equip participants with the knowledge and practical skills required for motor vehicle assessment. Offered in collaboration with key industry stakeholders, it serves as a prerequisite for individuals seeking recognition and licensing as certified motor assessors.

APPLY NOW

Insurance Agents Course (IAC)

This foundational professional competence course is offered in collaboration with the Pensions and Insurance Authority (PIA) and serves as a prerequisite for licensing as an insurance agent in Zambia, equipping participants with essential knowledge and skills for a successful career in the insurance industry.

APPLY NOW

Non Life Underwriting & Claims Management (UCM)

This specialized professional competence course is offered in collaboration with the Pensions and Insurance Authority (PIA) and is designed to enhance technical expertise in underwriting and claims handling for non-life insurance. It serves as a key requirement for professionals seeking licensing and career advancement in the general insurance sector.

APPLY NOW

Pensions Trustee (CCTOPS)

This professional competence course is offered in collaboration with the Pensions and Insurance Authority (PIA) and is a mandatory requirement for individuals serving as pension scheme trustees. It equips participants with the regulatory knowledge and fiduciary responsibilities necessary for effective governance and oversight of pension funds.

APPLY NOW

Occupational Health

This essential professional competence course is offered in collaboration with key industry and regulatory stakeholders and is designed to equip participants with the knowledge and skills needed to manage workplace health and safety. It serves as a foundational requirement for professionals seeking to promote employee well-being and ensure compliance with occupational health standards.

APPLY NOW

Certificate in ICT Skills
