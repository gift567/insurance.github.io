Certificate in Insurance

Course Information

  • Mode of Learning Online and Physical
  • Course Duration 4 Weeks
  • Course Start July 28, 2025

Description

The Certificate in Insurance is a foundational program designed to provide learners with essential knowledge and skills in the principles and practices of insurance. This course is ideal for individuals seeking to start a career in the insurance industry or those currently working in the field who wish to formalize their understanding and qualifications.

Students will explore how insurance operates as a financial service and risk management tool, and how it contributes to economic and social development. The program also emphasizes ethical standards, regulatory compliance, and the application of insurance principles in real-world scenarios.
